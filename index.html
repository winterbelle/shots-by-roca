About Michael

Michael, based in Brooklyn, NY, is an automotive photographer and videographer driven by a lifelong passion for car culture. As an active member of the scene himself, he brings an insider’s perspective to his work. His photography explores the relationship between performance vehicles and their environments, capturing not just the machines but the atmosphere surrounding them. Through both ground-level photography and aerial drone work, he documents the motion, atmosphere, and presence of the automotive scene, from street moments to organized events.
